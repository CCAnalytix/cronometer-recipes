LDL Overnight Oats with Yogurt (Anabolic Matrix Bowl)

Ingredients (1 serving)

  • 200 g Siggi's 4% plain Skyr (or any whole milk Greek yogurt)
  • 40 g Bob's Red Mill Organic Extra Thick Rolled Oats
  • 15 g ground flaxseed
  • 15 g walnut halves
  • 14 g sunflower seeds (hulled)
  • 100 g blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 50 g raspberries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 g ground cinnamon
  • 2 g unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 30 ml unsweetened soy milk (optional, for texture)

Instructions

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl or jar.
  2. Stir in oats, ground flaxseed, walnuts, sunflower seeds, cinnamon, cocoa powder, and soy milk (if using).
  3. Fold in blueberries and raspberries.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  5. Eat cold as a single high-fiber, lipid-optimizing breakfast bowl.
